# 🌍 CLAT Current Affairs & GK — Complete Study Guide

> **Section Weight**: 28–32 questions | ~25 marks | **25 minutes**  
> **Core Skill**: Understand **background, causes, and consequences** — not just superficial facts.

---

## How CLAT Tests GK & Current Affairs

| CLAT Tests | CLAT Does NOT Test |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Why** an event happened | Obscure dates with no context |
| **Impact** on India/policy | Trivia about minor celebrities |
| **Connections** between schemes and ministries | Rote memorisation without comprehension |
| **Cause-effect** in international relations | State-level news (unless nationally significant) |

### The 4-Bucket Compilation Strategy

Compartmentalise daily news into buckets — revise bucket-wise, not chronologically:

| Bucket | Content | Priority |
|--------|---------|----------|
| **1** | Government Schemes & Legislation | ⭐⭐⭐ Highest |
| **2** | SC Judgments & Constitutional Appointments | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **3** | International Summits & Treaties (India signatory) | ⭐⭐ |
| **4** | Macroeconomic Data (Budget, RBI, Indices) | ⭐⭐ |

## PART G: PRACTICE SET (15 Questions)

**Q1.** Which scheme provides 300 units of free electricity monthly to households?
**A**: PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana

**Q2.** What replaced the IPC from July 2024?
**A**: Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita (BNS)

**Q3.** India hosted which global summit in 2023 with the theme "One Earth, One Family, One Future"?
**A**: G20

**Q4.** Which amendment introduced Fundamental Duties?
**A**: 42nd Amendment (1976)

**Q5.** Kaziranga National Park is famous for which animal?
**A**: One-horned rhinoceros

**Q6.** Who is the current RBI Governor (as of 2025)?
**A**: Sanjay Malhotra

**Q7.** Chandrayaan-3 achieved soft landing on which part of the Moon?
**A**: South pole

**Q8.** What is India's fiscal deficit target for FY26 per Budget 2025-26?
**A**: 4.4% of GDP

**Q9.** Which river is known as "Dakshin Ganga"?
**A**: Godavari

**Q10.** COP29 (2024) was held in which city?
**A**: Baku, Azerbaijan

**Q11.** Which constitutional amendment introduced GST?
**A**: 101st Amendment (2016)

**Q12.** Gir National Park is the only habitat of which species?
**A**: Asiatic lion

**Q13.** What is the target year for Viksit Bharat?
**A**: 2047

**Q14.** Which movement was launched with the slogan "Do or Die"?
**A**: Quit India Movement (1942)

**Q15.** BRICS expanded in 2024 to include how many new members?
**A**: 5 (Egypt, Ethiopia, Iran, UAE, Saudi Arabia)
